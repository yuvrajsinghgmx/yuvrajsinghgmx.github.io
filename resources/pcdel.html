Data Deletion Request

Your Right to Data Deletion

At PatientCure, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Important: When we delete your account, we remove all personally identifiable information and medical data associated with your account. This action cannot be undone. You will lose access to your account history, saved reports, and any analysis results.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data in the following ways:

  1. Email Request: Send an email to yuvrajsinghgmx@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your account email address in the body.
  2. Web Form: Complete the form below, and we will process your request.

Data Deletion Request Form

What Happens Next

After submitting your request:

  • We will send a confirmation email to verify your identity
  • Once verified, we will process your deletion request within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email when your data has been deleted
  • Any data retained for legal compliance purposes will be deleted when no longer required

Data Retention for Legal Purposes

In some cases, we may need to retain certain information for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or other legitimate business purposes. Any such data will be minimized and securely stored with restricted access until it can be safely deleted.

Need Help? If you have any questions about the data deletion process or need assistance, please contact our privacy team at yuvrajsinghgmx@gmail.com.
