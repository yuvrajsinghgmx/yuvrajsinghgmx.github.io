Description:

CMT is a powerful, user-friendly application designed to streamline your email management. Whether you need to send customized emails, manage sent emails, or save drafts, CMT simplifies the process for personal and professional use. Seamlessly integrated with Firebase for secure cloud storage, CMT ensures that all your sent emails are stored and easily retrievable whenever needed.

With CMT, you can:

  • Quickly compose and send personalized emails
  • View and manage your sent emails, all in one place
  • Easily resend emails with a single tap
  • Enjoy a smooth, intuitive interface designed with Jetpack Compose

Key Features:

  • Email Management: Store, view, and manage all your sent emails.
  • Firebase Integration: Securely save your email history in the cloud for future access.
  • Customizable Interface: Intuitive design built using Jetpack Compose to provide a fluid user experience.
  • Secure & Private: Ensures that your data remains protected with Firebase's robust authentication system.
